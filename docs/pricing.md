## What counts as a Patch Install?

A **patch install** is counted each time a device downloads and applies a patch. If your app has 10,000 users and you push one patch, that's up to 10,000 patch installs.

## Overage

On Pro and Business plans, if you exceed your included patch installs, additional installs are billed at $1 per 2,500 installs.

## Self-hosted

Self-hosted deployments are free for non-commercial use. Commercial use requires a paid plan. See [Self-hosted](self-hosted.md) for setup instructions.
